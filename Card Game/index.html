How to Play

Objective: Match all the pairs of cards!

How to Play:

  • Click on cards to flip them and reveal the emoji
  • Try to find matching pairs
  • Match all pairs to win!
  • Use Hint, Undo, or Power-ups strategically

Game Modes:

  • Classic: Traditional matching game
  • Speed Run: Complete as fast as possible!
  • Daily Challenge: Same puzzle for everyone daily
  • Multiplayer: Take turns with another player

Difficulty Modes:

  • Normal: Unlimited flips - relax and play!
  • Easy: 30 flips allowed
  • Hard: 20 flips allowed
  • Insane: 10 flips allowed - for the brave!

Keyboard Controls: H=Hint, U=Undo, P=Pause, R=Reset

⏸ Game Paused

Press P or click Resume to continue
